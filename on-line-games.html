Guess A Number!

Guess A Number! is a simple guessing game that teaches logical thinking. The player must guess a randomly generated number between 1 and 100. An error is generated for numbers less than 1 and greater than 100. The game tells you if your guess is too low or too high so you can make another guess. The player gets 10 chances. There is a restart button. Select the Guess A Number! link above to begin playing
